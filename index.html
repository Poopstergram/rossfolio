Like his father, Ross likes to get his hands dirty. He torrented Photoshop and started designing at 13 so he could make promo materials for dad's business. He sold the website and bought a ticket to Japan after graduating highschool, returning 2 years later with fluent Japanese and half of body fat he went with.

Ross went to the Australian National University to study software engineering, making full use of the long summer breaks to do more travelling. Upon graduating, he gave up bar management to do front-end development for startups and big corporations in Sydney like American Express and Sydney Water.

In 2015 he attended [and won] his first hackathon, planting the seeds for the next chapter, Web Three. TBC.
